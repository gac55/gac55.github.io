R&D at Arup

I'm interested in evidence based decision making and modelling for enlightenment, not post-justification. I mostly work on city scale simulations, for both hard infrastructure scenario testing (e.g. new metro systems) and soft policy testing (e.g. road pricing). This involves research into synthetic populations, location device inference, activity based modelling and large scale graph computations (to reduce computational run times) for fine resolution simulations.
